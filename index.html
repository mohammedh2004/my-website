About Me

Hi, My name is Mohammed Hashash.

My journey in self-development began when I decided to learn English. I enrolled in ZamercanEnglish, the best platform for learning English online. It took me two years to learn the language to the point where I could write and speak it fluently. Then came the high school exams, and I succeeded with a score of 91%. After that, I chose not to enroll in university so I could work as an English teacher in public schools to gain more skills in communication and teaching. After finishing my school duties, I spent the rest of my time learning programming. I started with Python, then moved on to Frontend development. I began by learning HTML, then CSS, followed by the Tailwind framework. Afterward, I learned JavaScript and built projects at every stage. Currently, I am progressing to learn React. Later, I decided to study Information Technology Engineering (ITE) at the Syrian Virtual University (SVU), with my studies set to begin in August of this year. This decision will help me continue my education and improve myself further in the field of technology, which is essential in our modern era. Additionally, I remain committed to my passion for inspiring people to achieve their dreams. This has been, and still is, my ongoing journey toward fulfilling my dream.
